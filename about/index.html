About

So, hello friends, colleagues, subscribers, readers and visitors to my blog.

I created this blog because during the day/week/month I "absorb" so much information that it is somehow inappropriate to keep it all in notes on my phone and in notebooks that are already lying around the entire apartment.

I am not a programmer, not a professional blogger, I do not have tens of thousands of subscribers, today I do not even have a tik-tok😄 - I am a beginner, moreover, I do not have a specialty or any profession thanks to which I can earn "big" money. I work at a sawmill, hence the name of my site. I want to show and prove that absolutely anyone, it does not matter who you work for, can create your own website or blog and you can go through this path together with me.

In general, the last 68 days since I quit drinking alcohol, a lot of thoughts, ideas, goals, plans and even dreams have appeared. I will not describe it all here, brag about progress, because everyone has their own path. It will simply be a project of mine in which I learn something new, necessary and interesting for myself and I will share it here for free.

To make money, you need to create something, you need a product, and moreover, you need value, something that people will really pay their money for. Something that will attract a person's attention to make this purchase. To give value, you need to know something yourself.

Therefore, with the help of this blog, I will acquire new knowledge.

The topics that I have chosen for training, development and my attention are: HTML, CSS (maybe partly a little more in coding), SEO, SMM, Sales, AI and content-making (creation of digital products, images, videos, books), statistics, analytics, mathematics.

The main goal in principle will be learning and mastering new skills and gaining knowledge. I will also test different services and give a description.

If you are interested, be sure to subscribe, add to bookmarks, I welcome everyone, and I will be very happy to answer your questions and comments.

P.S. above I write about subscriptions, comments and other topics and sections of this site, do not pay attention that something is not possible, it means I have not done it yet. This project is like a free course on building a website with your own hands from scratch in real time.
